Corporate Aroma Identity

Turn Your Brand Into a Signature Experience.

At Taqder, we believe your brand should be more than seen — it should be felt. Our Aroma Identity service helps businesses create an unforgettable sensory connection with clients, employees, and visitors by introducing a signature scent that reflects your identity and values.

What is Aroma Identity?

Aroma Identity, or scent branding, is the art of creating a unique fragrance that represents your brand personality. Just like a logo or a color palette, a signature scent is a powerful branding tool that enhances emotional connection and brand recall.

Imagine walking into a space and instantly recognizing a brand by its scent. That's the kind of immersive experience we create for you.

Our Aroma Identity Solutions

We design and deliver custom fragrance concepts that are elegant, sophisticated, and memorable — perfectly tailored to your brand. Our solutions include:

Signature Fragrances: Custom-blended scents that align with your brand's values and environment.

Luxury Aroma Collections: Premium musk-based selections and oud blends for a rich, cultural impression.

Branded Diffusers & Sprays: Designed for offices, retail spaces, or corporate events with your logo and identity.

Gifting Integration: Incorporate your signature scent into business gifts, hampers, or onboarding kits for a cohesive brand experience.
